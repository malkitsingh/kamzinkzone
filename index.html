NICK SHARMA - the godfather ( Mentor of kamz)
Meet Nick Sharma. Nick is the guy who made Kamz what he is today.

Kamz the boss @KamzInkZone. Kamz was in 10th standard when he had first encounter with term tattoo and it was his friend Upkar who introduced this tattoo term. Kamz is trained by Nick Sharma and have attended many conventions in recent years.

Divinya

About Divinya

Divinya, one of Kamz student who came to Kamz in 2014, after completing her graduation in b.com. She used to paint in her school and college as well. When she showed her work to Kamz, he was pretty impressed by her. He asked if she had learnt arts from somewhere but she said it came pretty naturally to her. Divinya wanted to expand her horizon so she came to KAmz for learning tattoos. After 6 months training from Kamz, she started doing tattoos on human canvas. And now she is working with Kamz.

Mntri

About Mntri

Mntri too had this passion of art from his school days, he grew drawing various paintings throughout his school life. Kamz was Mntri's school time friend and he went to Kamz's studio in 2012 and he was inspired by Kamz's work.

Mntri joined Kamz Ink Zone full time in 2015 and since then he is exploring various new things with Kamz.

Meet the team

KamzInkZone is a team of three expert like minded tattoo artists. All of these artists share similar vision and does wonderful job in their own field of expertise.

Team consists of 3 tattoo artists, Kamz himself, Divinya and Mntri .
